LET'S TALK ABOUT US

We are a startup using Riot API and magic

to make your game experience better

Riot uses an algorithm to match you with your teammates on the same skill level (yes, toxic ones too). According to algorithm, every player has a MMR (Match Making Rating) calculated based on different factors. However, MMR system has its weaknesses. In traditional way, you may have the same MMR with a player who did not play for last 3 months, or a player who lost his/her last 9 games out of 10.


You can only climb ranks if everyone on the team want to win, and take action to climb the rank. You can't choose everyone in the Solo/Duo Queue. But you can choose one.

We are here to help you find the best teammate, listening for your needs.
