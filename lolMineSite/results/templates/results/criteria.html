AGGRESSION

Aggression is basically a calculation of DPS (Damage Per Second). We divide the total damage dealt to champions score to game duration.

You might want to give priority to this skill when you want an assassin mid or jungle player.

SURVIVAL

Survival is an indicator of ability to not die during game as long as possible. We divide longest time survived score to game duration.

Usually solo, tanky junglers and tanky supports are good at surviving. If you want to play with one of these types, survival is the option for you.

FARMING

Farming is actually refers to last-hit skills of your teammate. We divide total minions killed by game duration to get farming score.

Most players demand an ad carry with high minions score in game.

VISION

Vision is very important for macro strategies. Most of the time warding is done by the support. In high elo, everyone is responsible for vision.

We calculate vision score by simply dividing respective score to game duration again. Riot has a more complex algorithm for calculating vision score.
